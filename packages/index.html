    About Us

    Public Charitable Trust

    Who we are

    Vijai Foundation is a Public Charitable Trust started on 30th March, 2011 by the family of Vijai Kumar. The trust is based on the belief that good Health and Education are fundamental for every individual. Vijai foundation has since then been striving to achieve this through their various activities.

    Our History

    Genesis

    Vijai Foundation was born in the memory of Vijai Kumar. The family wanted to give back to the society in ways that bring hope and positivity in the lives of the underprivileged and weaker section of the society. Vijai Foundation started off organising various rural health camps on cancer awareness & consultation. In 2016, we ventured into education by starting Ezhichi, rural library with the aim to create a passion for reading. Reading is the first step towards learning for life.
