    Healthcare

    To promote an environment to reduce the risk of Cancer.

    Projects

    Cancer awareness camps

    • The aim of the awareness camps is to spread the knowledge on Cancer among the rural population so that they approach the doctors immediately when they notice any symptoms.
    • Home visits, distribution of cancer literature, display of posters at prominent location etc are also done.

    Early detection screening

    • We conduct screening camps to identity any symptoms. In cases of the early stage, with proper treatment management, the cancer can be completely cured.
    • We partner with government primary health centres in the villages to conduct the camps.
    • Doctors talk to patients to clarify their doubts, queries and fears.

    Patient assistance program

    • Cancer treatment can bring with it many unexpected expenses from medications to travel.
    • For the underprivileged, it is a huge burden sometimes to the point where they might compromise on their treatments.
    • To lessen their burden, we provide financial support to the needy patients.
    • We also partner with Meenakshi Mission Hospital to fund their children cancer ward.
